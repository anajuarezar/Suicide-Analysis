Data Processing

We followed a process with our data, which is described below:

It is important to mention that the data used in this project contains the period within the years ____
We decided to analyze suicide rate through a series of demographic, economical, geographical, and social factors.
Our intention is to be able to predict if a person is more likely to die from suicide if they have certain factors in their life. We used data from various resources such as world bank, our world in data, kaggle, and WHO. The data was downloaded from the sources mentioned above and then cleaned using python and pandas. Afterward, all the databases were merged together and became uniform. Data was also normalized between all variables.
We then processed the data through machine learning using models such as : linear regression, logistic regression, random forest, and support vector machine. Of course, there were differences in accuracy because not all models are fit for this kind of purpose: prediction. Some are general models to note relationship between data and other factors. If data is unfit for the type of model it causes faults in accuracy.
Beyond logical reasoning, we also found research that sustaining economical factors such as unemployment, had a strong influence on the suicide mortality rate. Other factors we noticed with a strong relationship were mental health and substance abuse and of course death rate.
We hope that with this research, awareness can be raised so additional funding is done in welfare-state policies that may reduce the suicide rate mortality.
